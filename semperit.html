Sweet Bake by 4N



Semperit

The taste is so wonderful and unique . The crunchy, creamy taste coupled with the sweet fat is a favorite among small children .

Instructions


Beat butter and icing sugar until white, then add eggs and vanilla essence. Beat until fluffy.


Add wheat flour, custard flour and corn flour. Stir and knead until it becomes a soft dough.


Pour the mixture into a springform pan and press it onto a greased baking tray.


Garnish with cherries on top.


Finally, bake in the oven at 180 Â°C for 10-15 minutes or until cooked.

Ingredients


260g wheat flour

350g butter

200g of icing sugar

200g of custard flour

100g corn flour

2 eggs

1 teaspoon vanilla essence

red cherries and green cherries for decoration
