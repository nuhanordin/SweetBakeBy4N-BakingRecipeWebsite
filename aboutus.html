About Us

Sweet Bake as comfort zone to you and us

SweetBakeBy4N was founded in October of 2021 as a way to connect with other people who love to bake and need a recipe guide.
The name of Sweet Bake By 4N is inspired by the combination from the first name letters of the founder for this website.
The founder is Nuha Nordin, Noornajwa Amirah, Nik Fatnin, and Nurizazi.

Sweet Bake By 4N is the trusted destination for bakers of all levels to learn, be inspired and share their enthusiasm for baking with those they love.
We want to create, educate and inspire you to become the best baker you can be, so we never show you a cake without helping you to make it, reveal a product without a recipe, or recommend anything that doesn't make you and your baking letter. So, good luck!!! Thank you for coming.
